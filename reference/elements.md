# Data frame of elements and their average weights

Gets a data.frame with all elements, their symbol, name, atomic number
and their abridged standard atomic weight according to the International
Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC).

## Usage

``` r
elements()
```

## References

Prohaska T, Irrgeher J, Benefield J, Böhlke JK, Chesson LA, Coplen TB,
et al. Standard atomic weights of the elements 2021 (IUPAC Technical
Report). Vol. 94, Pure and Applied Chemistry. 2022. p. 573–600.
http://dx.doi.org/10.1515/pac-2019-0603
